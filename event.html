R’IMPRESSARIO FIESTA

R'IMPRESSARIO FIESTA , hosted by The Commerce Society, Ryan International School, Rohini. Last year ,R’IMPRESSARIO , Comprised Of 7 events across fields, Business- Trade, Start-up Pitching, Mock stock, War Simulation, Fantasy - based IPL Auction, Quizzing, Treasure Hunt, and a Crime Mystery.

Virtually Conducted, We experienced and delivered our organizing service to over 750 participants across 61+ schools, from various locations in India.

One of the earliest Societies in the circuit to conduct an online-virtual commerce fest, we now plan to take forward the legacy, by bringing on board our next batch of dedicated commerce aspirants.

1 . THE MANGAL BAZAAR Richest Martian

The entire event is based on a storyline, which is set somewhere in the 22 century. The worldending apocalypse is near and people have started establishing colonies on the new earth, previously known as Mars. The participants( In teams of 1-2) will simulate themselves as the people who have successfully landed on Mars and now have to make it as big as they can! It involves all the stages seen in an economy’s growth, from being in its early stage to an affluent hyper city.

The participants are required to use their business and financial acumen to gain, sell and regulate their shares in THE MANGAL BAZAAR and come out as the ‘Richest Martian'.

2 . THE CONQUER Quest

Middle Earth is divided into three kingdoms-Mordor, Gondor and Andor. The three kingdoms have waged war upon each other. Each team would be a province in one of these kingdoms and each kingdom is rich in a particular combination of resources. Each participant will be randomly assigned a kingdom and would have to decide the name of their province. The resources will be given to them in a separate document and it is advised to keep it confidential. The geographical location of these kingdoms is surrounded by four types of terrains on which the battle will take place on

1. Mountains

2. Desert

3. Swamp

4. Plateau

3 . RYAN PREMIER League

This is an event which will test both bidding and trading skills. A CRICKET ATTACKS game session will be organized in the last round. Participants have to prepare their own cricket team, keeping in mind that their team should have the best players, according to the portfolio of the players. There will be 2 participants in each team or lone wolf is also allowed

4 . UDDYAM-E-GRAAM Event

The registered teams have to apply their business and marketing concepts in this event.There will be two participants in each team.

The participants will be given a product which will be coming from rural areas and they ned to prepare a business plan, including the branding sales promotion strategies for the product. Considering the current situation of Global Pandemic.
